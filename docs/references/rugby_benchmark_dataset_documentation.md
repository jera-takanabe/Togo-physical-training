# Rugby Physical Benchmark Dataset

This dataset provides benchmark values for youth rugby physical
performance testing. It is designed for development tracking and
motivational benchmarking.

## Tests Included

-   10m sprint (acceleration)
-   20m sprint (speed)
-   CMJ (countermovement jump)
-   Standing long jump (horizontal power)
-   Pro agility 5-10-5 (change of direction)
-   RSI (reactive strength index)
-   Medicine ball throw (upper body power)

## Benchmark Levels

general_youth_p50\
Typical population median for youth around age 11-13.

youth_athlete_p50\
Median level for trained youth athletes (soccer academies, youth rugby
etc.).

elite_u18_p50\
Approximate level for elite national-level U18 athletes.

world_elite_p50\
Professional elite performance reference values.

## Data Sources

The benchmark values are synthesized from sports science literature and
combine datasets including:

-   Youth sprint development studies
-   Rugby academy physical testing research
-   Soccer academy athletic performance research
-   NFL combine data
-   Strength & conditioning research literature

Example commonly cited sources:

-   Development of juvenile sprint performance in boys
-   Anthropometric and physical qualities of elite youth rugby players
-   Countermovement jump performance in rugby athletes
-   Determinants of standing long jump performance in youth
-   NFL combine agility benchmarks

## Usage

These benchmarks are intended for:

-   Development tracking
-   Motivational comparison
-   Domain score calculation

They should not be interpreted as strict performance requirements due to
growth and maturation differences in youth athletes.
